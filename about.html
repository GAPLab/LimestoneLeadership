Vision Statement

To have a broad and long lasting impact on various leaders around the globe by not settling (coach or client) but striving for growth.

Coaching Mission Statement

To make a positive impact on the clients I work with through coaching. The impact and success factors will be identified by and achieved by the client.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe that clients and colleagues have a lot to offer through their own training and experience. With this in mind I try to use participatory leadership in my training as this facilitates discussion among all participants and values input and collaboration by all. As the facilitator I then bring together what is discussed and work the group to cover what matters to the group. I also bring in my own knowledge, training and experience to deepen/enhance what is being discussed.

Approach/Methods

In any session I facilitate I like to talk with participants and not talk at. I believe in having a shared platform for learning. I strive to make sessions as interactive as possible to make the session more engaging, practical and relevant. I can also change my approach based on how the session is going to maximize outcomes for the group (talk more if needed or less).

Credentials

MSc, BA, in certification for CPCC through CTI. I am currently in the process of completing the requirements for becoming a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach (CPCC). Upon completion of CPCC, I will be applying for membership from the International Coach Federation, (ICF) which sets the accepted “gold standard” for professional credentials within the coaching industry.

Experience

I am working with a range of clients in various life stages. I often work with clients that are in transition. For example, clients who have recently finished school and are moving into the workforce.

Personal Values

Honesty, integrity, authenticity, curiosity, family, positivity, hope, and the journey.
