This OT Can

October 20, 2016

#ThisOTCan improve the quality of life of people who experience a serious mental illness and support them to achieve success in community living. Gord Unsworth is a community advocate, a manager with the Community High Intensity Treatment Team at Providence Care in Kingston, a leadership coach at Limestone Leadership, and lecturer at Queen's University. Through his work with Providence Care, Gord provides recovery-oriented treatment, rehabilitation, and support services to those who experience serious mental illnesses and who have complex needs. Through his team’s impactful work, individuals are able to achieve and maintain their goal of living in their communities. Gord’s experience working with individuals to effect the change they hope to see in their lives led him to recently pursue a certification in co-active coaching. Gord now uses his leadership coaching skills to equip others to become long-lasting change agents.
